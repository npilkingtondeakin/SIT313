Insomnia was a game me and my friend made where the objective is to get to sleep, but there are things in the house that keep you up at night that you need to disarm before you can get to sleep. It's about rushing to get as much sleep as you can before you run out of time, but if you arent careful you can end up burning your house down or getting burgled. The 'Insomnia' is represented as a boss fight that you need to defeat to win, the more preperation you do before going to bed makes the fight easier.'
